Domegle

Peer-to-Peer Decentralized Omegle

Domegle is an omegle like app that runs on a p2p network, which is open and decentralized in nature compared to the centralized omelge and other omegle like apps.

As usual, the service randomly pairs users in one-on-one chat sessions where they chat(text and video) anonymously using the names "You" and "Stranger".
